Method: The proposed STHTD-MP employs the symmetric part of the behavior-policy Bellman matrix to define the update geometry, integrates a single learning rate for variables, and uses a Mirror-Prox prediction-correction step.

The analysis shows that STHTD-MP can have a smaller mean contraction factor than GTD2-MP when the behavior-induced metric improves the saddle-point geometry.
